MATLAB scripts for temperature data analysis and figure generation. 
Temperature increase (ΔT) was defined as the difference between mean 
baseline temperature and mean temperature during the final 40 s of 
illumination. Heating rate was defined as mean ΔT during the first 20 s 
of illumination (°C/s). Statistical comparisons used two-way ANOVA with 
Tukey post-hoc correction (α = 0.05).
Scripts included:
- temp_statistics: ANOVA and Tukey analysis
- temp_figures: bar plots of ΔT and heating rate per fiber/probe
- temp_baseline_figure: baseline temperature visualization
- pulse_temp_figure: pulse-response figure (heating/cooling cycles)
